Selecting Spells from 5etools

1. Open your web browser and go to 5etools.
2. Navigate to the "Spells" section.
3. Browse through the spells and pin the ones you want to include in your spellbook by clicking the pin icon next to each spell.
4. Once you have pinned all your desired spells, go to "Book View" under your spell.
5. Click on the "Download as Markdown" button to download your list.
6. Upload the list as a .md file.
